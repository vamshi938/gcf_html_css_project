Culture

Tradition, Arts & Festivals

South India preserves a deep artistic heritage: classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam and Kathakali, Carnatic music with ancient ragas, and temple arts that merge sculpture and ritual. Festivals such as Onam, Pongal, Ugadi, and Sankranti are celebrated with fervor and feasts.

Cuisine

Food in South India is a symphony of rice, coconut, spices, tamarind, and seasonal produce: dosas, idlis, sambar, Chettinad curries, Kerala fish specialties. Meals are often served on banana leaves during festivals and ceremonies.

Craft, Daily Life & Markets

From the vivid silks of Kanchipuram or Mysore to hand-woven baskets, bronze lamps, temple carvings, and local markets — craft is woven into daily life. Temples, festivals, and village fairs all pulse with this living cultural fabric.
